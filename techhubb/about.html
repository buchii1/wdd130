ABOUT!

Buchii is currently serving as the CEO and of TechHubb, a startup tech firm situated just outside of Berkeley, California, USA. The company resumed operations in the month of September, 2019, with a total team of 3 people. 2 software engineers and a cybersecurity expert. Since the company's inception, there has been a lot of positive changes. We started out in an underground garage. Currently, we have been able to procure a state of the art building, that is properly furnished with the latest tech equipments, both audio and visual, office tables, conference hall, relaxation center, standup zone, among others. We now boast of a total number of 12 full-time employees, 10 part-time employees, and 6 interns. We have made remarkable process over the years, thanks to you all who have been using our services. We love you all our esteemed users!
