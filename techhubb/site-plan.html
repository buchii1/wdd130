Overview

Purpose

I want to build a technical support website. The features I want to add to the website are: A community support page whereby users can interact with each other and share ideas relating to tech. A dedicated login page whereby only authenticated users can have access to chat in the community hangout page. The website will also feature a tech blog page where I get to post latest tech trends and updates. The goal of the website is to promote the study and knowledge of tech by bringing the users closer to the trends going on in the digital space. Finally, by signing up and becoming a member of the community, the users get to build life-long and meaningful relationships.

Audience

My audience are all people who make use of and interact with technology for their day-to-day activities. This can be workers, programmers, stay at home mums and dad, and also people who are struggling on how to get into tech. I plan on expanding my audience as my website gains popularity.

Tech Blog

How to use images with technology for websites

  • Images with technology are particulary suited for use with websites, particulary sites that focus on technology, digital communication, information, or other related fields. Used as headers, images with technology should be bold and intersting.
  • This is a part of your website where you really want to grab some attention and bring your visitors into the experience of your page. Don't go vague here -- chose an image with familiar elements presented in a new or intersting way. Once you've moved your viewer into the page itself, your background images can be more vague and a bit more subtle -- choose abstract or muted images that won't distract from your primary content.
  • When you're pairing images with technology with your body text, you want images that relate to what you're talking about, so don't pick unrelated visuals. Find a way to summarize what you're saying in the text with a striking image. After all, a picture is worth a thousand words!
